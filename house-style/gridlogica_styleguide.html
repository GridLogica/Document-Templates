GridLogica.
Independent Research

GridLogica formalises the pedagogy of immersive and spatial computing. The identity carries precision, scholarly rigour, and a quiet authority — never decorative, always purposeful.

Design Motif

The grid references both the logical structure of research frameworks and spatial computing environments. It recurs as background texture, layout scaffolding, and implicit metaphor.

Voice & Tone
Use Precise, measured, evidenced. Scholarly without being opaque. First person singular ("I investigate…").
Avoid Buzzwords, hyperbole, marketing copy. Never "innovative solution" or "cutting-edge". Let the research speak.
Tone Confident but not self-aggrandising. Intellectual peer-to-peer. Grounded in lived practice and formal theory.
